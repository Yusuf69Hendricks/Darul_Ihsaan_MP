Our History

Darul Ihsaan Institute was formed in 1985 and one of its founder members was the late Imaam Aziz Essack. Darul Ihsaan Institute is a Community Based/ Faith-Based, Non-Profit Organization (NPO), as a Muslim organization we strive for the protection and preservation of religion, honor and character. Darul Ihsaan Institute work and function within the framework of the SHARI’ AH ‘Muslim law’ in accordance with the Holy Quran & Sunnah.


We aim to aid, establish and maintain Islamic institutions. To re-establish Islamic values and standards among all Muslims. To uphold the dignity of ALL and MUSLIMS, within the framework of the SHARI’ AH. We continuously strive to protect and promote the safety of woman and children — especially the values and standards pertaining to marriage and families. Darul Ihsaan Institute’s objectives are to work in collaboration with other organizations and in doing so create employment for the empowerment of people in sub-economic areas. We strive for unity of all Muslim communities. We are affiliated to the Tafelsig Council of Islamic Scholars (TCIS) with the Head Office being at our Institute.
